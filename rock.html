Company overview

In 2019 Tassat launched the world’s first blockchain-based real-time B2B payments platform, TassatPay.

Tassat Group: The Company
  • Founded in 2017
  • Based in New York’s Flatiron “tech” district
  • Won 2021 Google Cloud Award for Innovation in Financial Services
TassatPay: The Platform
  • Initial launch in 2019
  • Over $600 Billion in real-time transactions to date
  • Tassat has added Smart Contracts and Fedwire functionality to make TassatPay a one-stop-shop for B2B Payments.
The Digital Interbank Network
  • The world’s first private blockchain-based real-time B2B payments network operating entirely within the existing regulatory framework of the US banking system.
  • Comprised only of FDIC insured banks transacting real-time payments and performing other banking services between their commercial customers – 24 hours a day, 365 days a year, via private permissioned blockchain.
  • Launched on October 1, 2022

Our services

Instant Payments
Instantaneous payments 24/7/365 between participating banks with no limits on transaction sizes or volumes.
Private Permissioned Network
Secure real-time B2B payments across Member Banks. Private permissioned blockchain is accessible only to entities allowed to view the data and transactions.
Bank Compliance-Focused
All Member Banks have established identities and utilize banks’ existing know-your-customer (KYC) and anti-money laundering (AML) processes.
Real-Time Services
Member Banks are able to offer an unlimited range of secure, real-time services including Fedwire integration and Smart Contracts. Innovative use cases and new applications can be deployed and adopted swiftly and efficiently.
Compatible & Customizable
The Network is compatible with legacy banking systems, allowing Member Banks to give clients access to the latest innovations without having to redesign their systems.
Supports U.S. Banks
Banks are the backbone of the U.S. Economy. The Network operates for the benefit of U.S. regulated banks. The Network allows banks to target both local and large vertical markets.
Why The Digital Interbank Network?

Benefits to Member Banks include:

Real-Time payments
Secure, real-time payments, 24 hours a day, seven days a week, 365 days a year with NO Network-imposed limits on transaction sizes or volumes.
ESG Targets
The Network allows commercial banking customers to adopt blockchain technology while adhering to corporate ESG targets.
Latest innovations
The Network’s technology is compatible with legacy banking systems, allowing Member Banks to give clients access to the latest innovations without having to redesign or fully upgrade their systems.
Secure data
Private permissioned blockchain is secure – data is not stored on a public blockchain.
Vertical Markets
The Network allows Member Banks to target both local and large vertical markets.
Transfer
Each transfer on the Network is tied 1:1 to USD in segregated accounts at FDIC insured banks.
